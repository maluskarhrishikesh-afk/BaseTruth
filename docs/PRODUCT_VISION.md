# Product Vision

## Positioning

BaseTruth is the ground-truth engine for document integrity.

The product should answer three questions clearly:

1. What does this document say?
2. Does it look internally consistent?
3. Is there evidence that it was edited, forged, or manipulated?

## Target Industries

- banking and lending
- payments and fintech
- insurance claims
- hospitals and healthcare administration
- payroll and HR operations
- compliance teams and internal audit

## Product Principles

- evidence over black-box scoring
- portable reports over opaque dashboards
- modular detectors over monolithic pipelines
- domain-specific rules over one-size-fits-all heuristics

## First Product Shape

The MVP in this repository is a command-line and library-first product.

That keeps the system easy to validate before building:

- a hosted API
- a multi-tenant dashboard
- domain-specific template libraries
- investigator workflows and case management
